Supercapacitor manufacturing

Currently, several experimental graphene electrode manufacturing processes are under development. None are yet being used for mass-production of graphene, however.

An interesting note about graphene supercapacitors is that they can be manufactured without industrial-grade equipment. It's even possible to make these in your backyard. Clickhere to explore how a team of college kids attempted to make a graphene supercapacitor using materials found in a small materials science lab and around the dorms.
