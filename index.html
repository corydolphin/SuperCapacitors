Supercapacitors

Graphene supercapacitors are on the cutting edge of science. They have the potential to completely change how society uses and understands energy. While supercapacitors are rare and limited in use right now, it is possible, if not likely, that we will begin to see their effects in just five years, and wonder how we lived without them in twenty. In this website you will see how we believe graphene supercapacitors will change society throughout our lifetime.
